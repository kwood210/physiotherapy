What we can do for you?

We offer Sports Physiotherapy (Injury assessment and treatment) and Sports & Remedial Massage in Helsinki. We will use differing techniques including stretching and exercises with the aim to achieve pain relief by increasing physical exercise and improving movement patterns by getting to the reason of WHY!
Sports & Remedial Massage is effective to help reduce stress levels and we believe that a sports massage should not exceed 30 minutes as there is no evidence to suggest that any longer timeframe will benefit you.
