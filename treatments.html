Sports & Remedial Massage

A sports and remedial massage is very similar to a deep tissue massage as the same techniques are used. It will solely focus on the site of pain or injury site to reduce tension and pain. It focuses on the muscle to break down adhesions, the range of movement, symmetry, and quality of posture.

Sports Therapy and Physiotherapy

Injury assessment, treatment and exercise rehabilitation

If you have a sporting injury or an injury that involves your muscles, bones, or ligaments - a full injury assessment is necessary to determine the exact injury. In this assessment we will check the mechanism of injury (how it happened), the range of movement in area of injury and use any special tests necessary.
